How to Use the CGPA Calculator

Our CGPA calculator helps you track your academic progress over multiple semesters. Enter your Grade Points and Total Credits for each semester. You can add as many semesters as needed by clicking the "+ Add Semester" button. This tool is perfect for students who want to keep an eye on their academic performance and meet university requirements.
