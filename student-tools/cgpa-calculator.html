How to Use the CGPA Calculator

Calculating your CGPA is crucial for tracking your academic performance. Our tool makes it simple and accurate:

  • Current CGPA: If you have already completed some semesters, enter your current CGPA and the total credit hours earned so far.
  • New Semester Data: Add your current semester's grade points and credit hours to see how it impacts your cumulative score.
  • Scale Support: Our calculator works with standard 4.0 and 5.0 GPA scales used by most universities.
  • Instant Results: See your updated CGPA immediately as you enter your marks.

Stay on top of your academic goals with our free and easy-to-use CGPA tool.
