How to Use the Study Timer

Enhance your productivity with our adjustable study timer. Set your desired session duration (default is 25 minutes), start the clock, and focus on your tasks until the alarm sounds. Use the break features to rest your mind effectively. This Pomodoro technique is statistically proven to help students and professionals maintain high levels of focus throughout the day.
