How to use the Pomodoro Study Timer

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method developed by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980s. It uses a timer to break work into intervals, traditionally 25 minutes in length, separated by short breaks. Use this timer to stay focused and improve your study productivity.
