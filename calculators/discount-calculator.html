How to Use the Online Discount Calculator

Our discount calculator makes shopping smarter. Here is how you can use it to find the best deals:

  • Original Price: Enter the price written on the tag before any deductions.
  • Discount Percentage: Enter the percentage off (e.g., 20, 50).
  • Instant Breakdown: The tool instantly shows you exactly how much money you save and what you'll actually pay.
  • Comparison: Use it to compare different sales and ensure you're getting a true bargain.

It's perfect for browsing online stores or calculating prices while at the mall.
