How to Use the BMI Calculator

Calculate your Body Mass Index (BMI) instantly. Choose your measurement system (Metric or Imperial), enter your height and weight, and click calculate. Our tool will categorize your BMI (Underweight, Normal, Overweight, or Obese) and provide the healthy weight range for your height, helping you monitor your health goals effectively.
