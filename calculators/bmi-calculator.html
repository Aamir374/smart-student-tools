How to Use the Online BMI Calculator

Tracking your Body Mass Index is a simple way to monitor your physical health. Our tool makes it easy:

  • Choose Units: Switch between Metric (kg/cm) and Imperial (lbs/ft/in) systems.
  • Enter Details: Provide your height and weight accurately for the best results.
  • Result Analysis: The calculator instantly shows your BMI value and categorizes it (e.g., Normal, Overweight).
  • Health Insights: See which category you fall into to help guide your fitness and wellness goals.

Please note that BMI is a general indicator and doesn't account for muscle mass or overall body composition.
