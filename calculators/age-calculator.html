How to Use the Age Calculator

Calculate your exact age in a few clicks. Simply select your Date of Birth from the calendar and choose the target date (defaults to today). Our tool will instantly show your age in years, months, and days, along with the total number of days you've lived—making it perfect for verifying eligibility for exams, jobs, or simply sharing with friends.
