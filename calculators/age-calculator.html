How to Use the Online Age Calculator

Our age calculator provides a detailed breakdown of your life timeline. Here is how you can use it:

  • Select Date of Birth: Use the calendar picker to choose the day you were born.
  • Target Date: By default, it calculates your age as of today, but you can choose a past or future date to see how old you were or will be.
  • Detailed Breakdown: The tool instantly shows your age in years, months, and days.
  • Total Days: See exactly how many total days have passed since your birth—great for milestones!

Perfect for filling out official forms, checking exam eligibility, or just satisfying your curiosity.
