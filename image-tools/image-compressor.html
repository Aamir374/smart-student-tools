How to Use the Online Image Compressor

Optimizing your images is essential for fast websites and saving disk space. Here's how to use our tool:

  • Upload Your Photo: Select any JPG or PNG image that you want to shrink.
  • Adjust Quality: Use the slider to choose your preferred balance between quality and file size.
  • Compare Results: See the original file size versus the compressed size in real-time.
  • Download & Save: Download the optimized version to use in your projects or social media.

This tool is perfect for students who need to upload assignments with strict size limits.
