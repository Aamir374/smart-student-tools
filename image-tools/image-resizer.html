How to Use the Image Resizer

Our online image resizer allows you to change the dimensions of your photos without losing much quality. Upload your image (JPG or PNG), enter your desired width or height, and our tool will handle the rest. It's specifically useful for resizing images for school projects, social media uploads, or website optimization, ensuring they meet specific size requirements.
